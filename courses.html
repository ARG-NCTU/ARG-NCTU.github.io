Welcome to Duckietown NCTU

Duckietown NCTU is the first branch of the MIT Duckietown (http://duckietown.mit.edu). We offer a graduate-level course Robotic Vision and a undergraduate-level course Creative Software Project in NCTU. Both courses aim at hands-on projects focusing on self-driving vehicles or assistive technology. Please see the detail in http://duckietown.nctu.edu.tw.
